## Code Decisions & Architecture Notes

- **`enable_validation_layers(true)` chosen over `request_validation_layers`**: hard-fail on machines without Khronos layer installed.
- **Surface created before physical device selection**: `vkb::PhysicalDeviceSelector` takes the surface at construction to enforce presentation support.
- **`VkPhysicalDevice` and `VkQueue` not destroyed in `destroy()`**: enumerated handles, not owned.
